About Me

Summer Healey is a burgeoning web developer and rehabilitated market researcher who is looking to help small businesses gain the competitive edge in their marketing and operations through responsive web design.

Before transitioning into web development, Summer spent the past five years working in a variety of industries developing tools and processes for customer service operations and brand marketing. After working in a variety of industries and positions, she found her passion for developing tools and resources for small businesses and now is completing UNC's Coding Bootcamp to develop the skills to deploy these tools across platforms.

Summer enjoys trying new games (Cards Against Humanity, anyone?), tap dancing, and learning about new programming languages.

Summer is available for freelance web development and customer service strategy consulting. You can reach Summer by using the contact form provided.
